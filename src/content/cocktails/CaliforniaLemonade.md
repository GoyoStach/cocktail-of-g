## Ingredients

- 2 oz of Bourbon Whiskey
- 1 oz of Fresh Lemon Juice
- 1/2 oz of Simple Syrup
- 4 oz of Club Soda
- Lemon wheel for garnish

## Instructions

In a highball glass filled with ice, combine bourbon whiskey, lemon juice, and simple syrup. Stir well. Top with club soda and gently stir. Garnish with a lemon wheel and serve immediately.
